BLT

Description

This BLT is classic: bacon, lettuce, and tomato â€” nothing fancy, just delicious. (I use fake vegetarian bacon these days, and it's still really good!)

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 2 leaves lettuce
  • 2 slices tomato
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Cook bacon in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on a paper towel-lined plate
  3. Arrange cooked bacon, lettuce and tomatoes slices on one slice of bread. Spread mayonnaise on the other slice of bread.
